Why CalledOut Music?

Why is CalledOut Music my favourite musician?

CalledOut Music has become one of the UK’s most sought after musicians. He has submerged fans across various geographical, demographic and socio-cultural backgrounds. This is also showcased in the diversity, range and style his music captures. More intentionally, his music captures the essence of faith and hope, spreading joy and positivity to all who come in contact with him.

He started the Gospel ministry 'Called Out' with the aim to 'get everyone to have the belief or mindset that they are called out for service to God'.
